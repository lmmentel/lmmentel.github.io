Automating slide presentations

From a prototype to final product in seconds

Delivering an interesting and engaging talk is hard.

Creating beautiful and visually appealing presentations is undoubtedly an art. The process is usally not so straightforward since there a lot of moving parts, formats. My weapon of choice was always LaTeX equipped with beamer that does an amazing job in figuring out some of the aesthetics for you. However to be honest most of the standard beamer themes offered do not look very modern and to say the least are far away from being stunning. Potentially this can easily be be fixed since LaTeX is extremely flexible and configurable but would require a peculiar set of skills namely graphic design and high level of proficiency in LaTeX internals. I guess no one should be surprized that those two rarely come together in a form that would benefit a larger community.

The main idea is to automate as much of the process as possiblee, so that once everything is set up and configured you can focus only on creating the actual content
