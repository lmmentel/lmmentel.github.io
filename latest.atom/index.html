The structural units characterising pores are usually rings from which the tube-like channels are formed (as shown below) and therefore the as described as <em>n</em> membered rings or <em>n</em>MR in short. It should also be taken into account that for most of the interesting applications topologies with MR &lt; 8 are not intersting since molecules cannot enter the small channels and channels larger than 14 would have very similar properties.</p> <p>IUPAC classification into nano, micro and mesoporous structures in a <a href="https://doi.org/10.1351/pac199466081739">technical report</a>.</p> <p>It is customary to call 8, 10 and 12MR, <em>small</em>, <em>medium</em> and <em>large</em> pore zeolites suggesting that this classification provides distinct groups of similar characteristing being size.
