Welcome!

I'm a theoretical chemist working currently in th Catalysis Group at the University of Oslo in Norway. The main focus of my current scientific efforts if to develop quantitative models relating the structure of the zeotype acid catalysts to their catalytic activity.

I did my PhD in the Section of Theoretical Chemistry at the VU University Amsterdam in the Netherlands.

I am also interested in the topic probem of optimal gaussian basis sets for molecular calculations of energies and various properties. To simplify the handling of basis sets and coenversions between formats of different quantum chemical programs I've developed a python module called basis set together with a convenient wrapper for optimizing the exponents of primitive funcitons and plotting completeness profiles. You can find more information in the software section or at the chemtools project webpage.

I am also a python enthusiast
